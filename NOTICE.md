## OpenCV Zoo models added for the classroom demo

The classroom demo includes three unmodified ONNX model files downloaded from
the official OpenCV Zoo repository:

- `models/person/object_detection_nanodet_2022nov.onnx` (NanoDet, Apache 2.0);
- `models/face/face_detection_yunet_2023mar.onnx` (YuNet, MIT);
- `models/face/face_recognition_sface_2021dec.onnx` (SFace, Apache 2.0).

The corresponding license texts are stored beside the model files. OpenCV Zoo
source: `https://github.com/opencv/opencv_zoo`.

The five reference portraits in `data/students/` were supplied by the project
owner for this private classroom demonstration. They are biometric/personal
data and must not be published without the subjects' permission.
